Caparezza

The artist who makes your mind travel with his rhymes

Here's a time line of Caparezza's life and music career:

  • 1973 - Caparezza ("Curly hair" in Apulian dialect, Michele Salvemini's artist name) was born in Molfetta, in the region of Apulia.
  • 1995 - Attended to the the Young Talents of Sanremo festival with the artist name of Mikimix.
  • 2000 - Released his first album ?! as Caparezza where he refuses the songs he made under the stage name of Mikimix.
  • 2003 - Second album Verit√† supposte (Supposed Truths) is out. He talks about the social problems of Italian society, particularly the Southern one, the entertainment in clubs and TV program with Sono fuori dal tunnel (I'm out of the tunnel) and racism with Io vengo dalla Luna (I come from the moon).
  • 2006 - Released Habemus Capam which deals with the contradictions of society and the marginalization of a teenage boy in which Caparezza identifies himself with La mia parte intollerante (My Intolerant Side).
  • 2008 - Le dimensioni del mio caos (The Size of my Chaos) is the fourth Caparezza's studio album where he discusses environmental issues about his region and security issues on the workplace with Eroe, Storia di Luigi delle Bicocche (Hero, History of Luigi delle Bicocche).
    In the same year, he published a book entitled Saghe mentali (Mind Saga) in which describes his discography in a humorous way.
  • 2011 - The fifth studio album Il sogno eretico (The Heretic Dream) is out. With this album he wants to involve his listeners to leave the dogma behind them and embrace critical thinking. Goodbye Malinconia deserves attention for its collaboration with Tony Hadley of Spandau Ballet and the issue of people fleeing abroad to look for a job and a better future.
  • 2014 - Museica is the sixth album whose name is composed by three words: musica (music), museo (museum) and sei (six). He uses art as a way to escape from the violence of the world and portrays the songs as a museum.
  • 2015 - Discovers to have the tinnitus which will influence the creation of following album.
  • 2017 - After going through a depression period, he made Prisoner 709 which is a completely different artwork from the previous ones. The dark-themed environment dominates him and the tracks and gives room to issues like the self-emprisonment of the mind.
  • 2021 - Last album Exuvia is considered the sequel of Prisoner 709, because he underlines the escape from the mental prison
